Six areas

WHAT WE
LOOK AT.

Every audit covers six areas of your digital presence, each scored and ranked by impact. You will know not just what is broken, but how much it is likely costing you and what to fix first.

01
Email list health
List size, engagement rate, deliverability, platform setup, segmentation, and whether your automated flows are running. Most businesses are surprised by how much is sitting idle.
02
Website performance
Load speed, mobile experience, Core Web Vitals, broken links and forms, accessibility basics. A slow or broken website is losing you customers before they get to the menu.
03
Conversion audit
Booking flow, menu access, contact clarity, trust signals, call-to-action effectiveness. Are visitors converting into customers — and if not, exactly where are they leaving?
04
Google presence
Business Profile completeness, local SEO ranking, review response rate, photo quality, hours accuracy. Your Google profile is often the first thing a potential customer sees.
05
Local search
Whether your business appears in local search results for the terms your customers are actually using. If you are not on the first page, most people will never find you.
06
Competitor benchmark
How your digital presence compares to two or three direct competitors in your area. You will know exactly where you are ahead, where you are behind, and what is worth closing the gap on.
