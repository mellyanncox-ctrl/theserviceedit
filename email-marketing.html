Automated flows

EMAILS THAT
RUN WITHOUT
YOU.

Every flow is triggered by customer behaviour — not by you remembering to send something. Once built, they work around the clock.

Welcome series
Sent when someone joins your list. Introduces your story, sets expectations, and gives new subscribers a reason to visit or order.
Win-back sequence
Triggered when a customer has not visited or ordered in a set period. Reminds them you exist and gives them a reason to return.
Post-visit follow-up
Sent after a booking or visit. Thanks them, invites feedback, and plants the seed for the next visit.
Abandoned cart recovery
For online businesses — sent when someone adds items but does not complete their order. Recovers a significant percentage of otherwise lost revenue.
Post-purchase sequence
For online businesses — follows up after an order. Encourages reviews, introduces complementary products, and builds repeat purchase behaviour.
Birthday or milestone flow
Triggered on a customer's birthday or anniversary with your business. One of the highest open-rate emails any hospitality business can send.
