Whether you are thinking about email, a new website, or both. The audit tells you exactly what is broken and in what order to fix it. It means every dollar you spend after it goes to the right place.

Six areas, each scored and ranked by impact:

  • Email list health. Size, engagement rate, deliverability, segmentation gaps
  • Website performance. Load speed, mobile experience, Core Web Vitals
  • Conversion audit. Booking flow, menu access, contact clarity, trust signals
  • Google presence. Business Profile, local SEO, review response rate
  • Social and content. Consistency, platform fit, missed channels
  • Competitor benchmark. How you compare to 2-3 direct competitors
