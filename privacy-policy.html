PRIVACY
POLICY.

Last updated: April 2025

WHO WE ARE

The Service Edit is a digital marketing consultancy operated by Mel Cox as a sole trader, ABN 88 429 285 575, based in Perth, Western Australia. We provide email marketing, digital audits and website builds for independent hospitality businesses.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@theserviceedit.com.

WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal information only when you provide it directly to us. This includes:

  • Your name, email address and business name when you complete our contact form or digital health check quiz
  • Your website URL if you provide it via the contact form
  • Your quiz responses and score from the digital health check
  • Any information you include in messages sent to us

We do not collect sensitive information as defined under the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and assess how we can help your business
  • Send you your digital health check results
  • Add you to our email list if you have opted in, and send you occasional marketing communications about our services and relevant content
  • Improve our website and services

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. We will never share it with anyone other than the service providers listed below who help us operate our business.

WHO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION WITH

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and communications:

  • Klaviyo — email marketing platform. Your email address and name are stored in Klaviyo when you subscribe to our list. Klaviyo is based in the United States and complies with applicable data protection laws.
  • Web3Forms — contact form processing. Enquiries submitted via our contact form are processed through Web3Forms and delivered to our inbox.
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages.

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

EMAIL MARKETING

If you subscribe to our email list via the contact form, the digital health check quiz, or our journal newsletter signup, you will receive occasional emails from us about our services and relevant content for hospitality businesses.

Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time. We will remove you from our list promptly on request.

HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. Our website is served over HTTPS. Access to personal information is limited to Mel Cox and the third-party providers listed above.

YOUR RIGHTS

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct information that is inaccurate, out of date or incomplete
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@theserviceedit.com. We will respond within a reasonable time.

COOKIES AND ANALYTICS

Our website does not currently use cookies or analytics tracking beyond what is standard to GitHub Pages hosting. If we introduce analytics tools in future, we will update this policy accordingly.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

CONTACT US

If you have a question or complaint about how we handle your personal information, please contact us at hello@theserviceedit.com. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au.
